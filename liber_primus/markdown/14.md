### Method

* Using a Vigenere cipher with the key `firfumferenfe` (`ᚠᛁᚱᚠᚢᛗᚠᛖᚱᛖᚾᚠᛖ`)
* Run the entire text (Page 14-15) as one continuous chunk through the cipher
* Skip the shift on the following runic indexes
    * 65, 76
